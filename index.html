The Aggression Typology

A character placement experiment

This instrument places you on a two-axis model of aggression style — impulsivity (how quickly threat or frustration translates into action) and influence (how much leverage you bring to a confrontation). A third dimension — activation — measures how readily you escalate from baseline toward action. Answer honestly; there are no good or bad quadrants, only different profiles. Each result maps to a film character who exemplifies that quadrant.

Your Profile

This is a conceptual instrument, not a clinical assessment. It measures self-perceived tendencies in a small number of dimensions and should not be interpreted as a diagnosis of anything.
